Our Story

A Centre Built on Care & Commitment

At Laxmi Tution Centre, we believe every child deserves patient, personalised attention. Founded by Mrs. Laxmi Ramatchandirane, our centre has been a trusted second home for students across CBSE and Stateboard in Pondicherry.

From class 3 right up to class 12, we cover all subjects — with special focus on higher secondary students tackling Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, and Accountancy.
